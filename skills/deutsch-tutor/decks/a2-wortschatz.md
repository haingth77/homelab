# A2 Wortschatz — Từ vựng trung cấp

## Arbeit & Beruf (Công việc)

Q: Was bedeutet "der Beruf"?
A: Nghề nghiệp (maskulin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Büro"?
A: Văn phòng (neutral)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Chef / die Chefin"?
A: Sếp (nam/nữ)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Kollege / die Kollegin"?
A: Đồng nghiệp (nam/nữ)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "die Besprechung"?
A: Cuộc họp (feminin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Termin"?
A: Cuộc hẹn / lịch hẹn (maskulin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "die Erfahrung"?
A: Kinh nghiệm (feminin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "verdienen"?
A: Kiếm tiền / xứng đáng

---

Q: Dịch: "Tôi làm kỹ sư."
A: Ich bin Ingenieur. (không dùng Artikel trước nghề nghiệp)

---

## Gesundheit (Sức khỏe)

Q: Was bedeutet "der Arzt / die Ärztin"?
A: Bác sĩ (nam/nữ)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Krankenhaus"?
A: Bệnh viện (neutral). Krank (ốm) + Haus (nhà)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "die Apotheke"?
A: Nhà thuốc (feminin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Rezept"?
A: Đơn thuốc / công thức nấu ăn (neutral)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "Kopfschmerzen"?
A: Đau đầu. Kopf (đầu) + Schmerzen (đau)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Fieber"?
A: Sốt (neutral)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "sich erkälten"?
A: Bị cảm lạnh (động từ phản thân)

---

Q: Dịch: "Tôi bị đau đầu."
A: Ich habe Kopfschmerzen.

---

Q: Dịch: "Tôi cần đi bác sĩ."
A: Ich muss zum Arzt gehen.

---

## Reisen (Du lịch)

Q: Was bedeutet "der Flughafen"?
A: Sân bay (maskulin). Flug (chuyến bay) + Hafen (cảng)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Bahnhof"?
A: Nhà ga (maskulin). Bahn (đường sắt) + Hof (sân)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "die Fahrkarte"?
A: Vé (tàu/xe) (feminin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Gepäck"?
A: Hành lý (neutral)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "die Unterkunft"?
A: Chỗ ở (feminin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Reisepass"?
A: Hộ chiếu (maskulin)

---

Q: Dịch: "Một vé đi Berlin, xin vui lòng."
A: Eine Fahrkarte nach Berlin, bitte.

---

Q: Dịch: "Tàu đi lúc mấy giờ?"
A: Wann fährt der Zug ab?

---

## Einkaufen (Mua sắm)

Q: Was bedeutet "der Laden" / "das Geschäft"?
A: Cửa hàng

---

Q: Was bedeutet "die Kasse"?
A: Quầy thu ngân (feminin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Preis"?
A: Giá (maskulin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "billig"?
A: Rẻ

---

Q: Was bedeutet "teuer"?
A: Đắt

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Angebot"?
A: Ưu đãi / lời đề nghị (neutral)

---

Q: Dịch: "Cái này giá bao nhiêu?"
A: Was kostet das? / Wie viel kostet das?

---

C: Das T-Shirt ist zu [teuer]. Gibt es etwas [Billigeres]? (đắt, rẻ hơn)

## Wetter (Thời tiết)

Q: Was bedeutet "Es regnet"?
A: Trời mưa

---

Q: Was bedeutet "Es schneit"?
A: Trời có tuyết

---

Q: Was bedeutet "Die Sonne scheint"?
A: Trời nắng (mặt trời chiếu sáng)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "Es ist bewölkt"?
A: Trời nhiều mây

---

Q: Was bedeutet "der Wind"?
A: Gió (maskulin)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "das Gewitter"?
A: Giông bão (neutral)

---

Q: Dịch: "Hôm nay thời tiết thế nào?"
A: Wie ist das Wetter heute?

## Häufige Verben (Động từ thường dùng)

Q: Was bedeutet "brauchen"?
A: Cần

---

Q: Was bedeutet "glauben"?
A: Tin / nghĩ rằng

---

Q: Was bedeutet "versuchen"?
A: Thử / cố gắng

---

Q: Was bedeutet "erklären"?
A: Giải thích

---

Q: Was bedeutet "vergessen"?
A: Quên (động từ mạnh: vergisst, vergaß, vergessen)

---

Q: Was bedeutet "sich freuen"?
A: Vui mừng (động từ phản thân). "Ich freue mich" = Tôi vui

---

Q: Was bedeutet "sich interessieren für"?
A: Quan tâm đến. "Ich interessiere mich für Musik" = Tôi quan tâm đến âm nhạc

---

C: Ich [freue] mich [auf] das Wochenende. (sich freuen auf + Akk = mong đợi)

---

C: Er hat seinen Schlüssel [vergessen]. (vergessen, Perfekt)
