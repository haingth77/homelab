# Writing Task 2 — Kỹ năng viết luận IELTS

## Essay Types (5 dạng bài chính)

Q: Name the 5 IELTS Writing Task 2 essay types.
A: 1) Opinion (Agree/Disagree) 2) Discussion (Both views) 3) Problem/Solution 4) Advantages/Disadvantages 5) Two-part question

---

Q: Structure for "Discuss both views and give your opinion"?
A: Intro (paraphrase + thesis) → Body 1 (View A + evidence) → Body 2 (View B + evidence) → Your opinion + conclusion. Mẹo: luôn nêu rõ ý kiến cá nhân.

---

Q: Structure for "To what extent do you agree or disagree"?
A: Intro (paraphrase + clear position) → Body 1 (Reason 1 + evidence) → Body 2 (Reason 2 + evidence) → Body 3 (optional: concession) → Conclusion (restate position). Mẹo: chọn rõ một phía, đừng "half-agree."

---

Q: Structure for "Problem/Solution" essay?
A: Intro (paraphrase + outline) → Body 1 (Problems + examples) → Body 2 (Solutions + examples) → Conclusion (summary + future outlook)

---

Q: What is the PEIC paragraph structure?
A: Point → Evidence → Impact → Conclusion. Giống SRE postmortem: Problem → Root cause → Impact → Resolution.

---

## Cohesive Devices (Liên kết đoạn văn)

Band 5-6 dùng: And, But, So, Also, Because. Band 7-8 dùng những từ dưới đây.

Q: Replace "And" / "Also" with Band 7-8 cohesive devices.
A: Furthermore / Moreover / In addition / Additionally / Likewise / Similarly

---

Q: Replace "But" with Band 7-8 cohesive devices.
A: However / Nevertheless / Nonetheless / Conversely / On the other hand / In contrast

---

Q: Replace "So" / "Because" with Band 7-8 cohesive devices.
A: Consequently / As a result / Therefore / Hence / Owing to / Due to the fact that / On account of

---

Q: What cohesive devices introduce an example?
A: For instance / For example / To illustrate / A case in point is / This is exemplified by

---

Q: What cohesive devices introduce a concession?
A: Admittedly / While it is true that / Despite / Notwithstanding / Although / Even though / Granted that

---

C: The policy has [undoubtedly] improved public safety. [Nevertheless], its implementation has [given rise to] concerns about privacy. (Addition → Concession flow)

---

## Thesis Statements (Câu luận điểm)

Q: Write a thesis for: "Should governments invest more in public transport?"
A: "This essay argues that increased government investment in public transportation is essential, as it reduces congestion and lowers carbon emissions." (Clear position + 2 reasons previewed)

---

Q: Write a thesis for: "Discuss both views on working from home."
A: "While remote work offers greater flexibility and work-life balance, it can also lead to social isolation and reduced collaboration. This essay will examine both perspectives before arguing that a hybrid model represents the optimal solution."

---

Q: What makes a strong thesis statement?
A: 1) Takes a clear position 2) Previews main arguments 3) Is specific (not vague) 4) Is one sentence. Mẹo: giám khảo nên đoán được cấu trúc bài từ thesis.

---

## Introduction Templates

Q: Write an introduction template for Opinion essays.
A: "[Paraphrase the topic]. While some argue that [opposing view], I firmly believe that [your position] because [reason 1] and [reason 2]."

---

Q: Write an introduction template for Discussion essays.
A: "[Paraphrase topic]. This is a contentious issue, with proponents arguing [view A] while opponents contend [view B]. This essay will examine both perspectives before presenting my own view."

---

C: In recent years, the [proliferation] of artificial intelligence has [sparked] a heated debate about its impact on employment. While some [contend] that AI will [displace] a significant proportion of the workforce, others [maintain] that it will [generate] new opportunities. (Band 8 intro example)

---

## Conclusion Templates

Q: How to write a Band 7-8 conclusion?
A: 1) Restate position (different words) 2) Summarize key arguments (1 sentence) 3) Future implication or recommendation. Length: 2-3 sentences. KHÔNG thêm ý mới.

---

C: In conclusion, while technology has [undeniably] transformed education, it is [imperative] that governments ensure equitable access to prevent a [widening] digital divide. Only through [concerted] efforts can society [harness] its full potential. (Band 8 conclusion)

## Common Task 2 Topics — Practice Prompts

Q: Technology topic: "Some people think AI will make many jobs redundant. Others believe it will create new types of employment. Discuss both views."
A: Discussion essay. View A: automation replacing routine jobs (manufacturing, data entry). View B: new roles (AI trainers, prompt engineers, ethics officers). Own view: net positive but requires reskilling investment.

---

Q: Environment topic: "The most effective way to reduce pollution is to tax companies that cause it. To what extent do you agree?"
A: Opinion essay. Agree: financial incentive, polluter pays principle, revenue for green tech. Partial concession: tax alone insufficient without regulation and public awareness. Use PEIC for each body paragraph.

---

Q: Education topic: "University education should be free for all students. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
A: Opinion essay. Position + reasons + examples. Consider: economic equality, brain drain prevention, government budget constraints, quality concerns.

## Error Patterns — Vietnamese Speakers

Q: Common Vietnamese error: "According to my opinion" — correct version?
A: "In my opinion" / "From my perspective" / "I believe that" (KHÔNG nói "According to me/my opinion")

---

Q: Common Vietnamese error: "Nowadays, more and more people..." — Band 8 version?
A: "In contemporary society, an increasing number of individuals..." / "In recent years, there has been a growing tendency to..." (Tránh "nowadays" và "more and more" — quá phổ biến)

---

Q: Common Vietnamese error: run-on sentences with "and...and...and" — fix?
A: Break into separate sentences or use varied connectors. "The population is growing. Consequently, housing prices are rising, which has led to increased homelessness."
